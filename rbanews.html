RBANews App Prototype

// November 2016

RBANews is a news app prototype that aims to provide a mobile platform for articles and newsletters that sat in the RBA Intranet. This was built predominantly in Sketch and translated into Principle for a working, animated prototype.


Planned features in the app included article filtering based on category, category creation, saving articles for offline viewing and comments.


The prototype tied for first place in the 2016 RBA Innovation competition and was awarded #1 Best Design.
