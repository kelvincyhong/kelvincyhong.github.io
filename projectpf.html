Project Passionfruit Logo Design

// January 2018

Project Passionfruit is an initiative that aims to inspire individuals to take action on their passions through thought provoking videos.


Based primarily on Youtube and Facebook, the video topics range from facing your fears, to how to live in the moment and be more productive.


The logo was created from the concepts of passion and passionfruit. The heart represents that passion is from the heart, and the fruit is shaped as a speech bubble to symbolise the mission of spreading the word.
