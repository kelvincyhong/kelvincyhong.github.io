AtlasBar logo design

Jan 2019

As part of a Hackathon alongside 4 developers, I helped design and created mocks of AtlasBar - an add-on that allows for Atlassian products to be easily accessed through the Touch Bar.


The logo was created from the concept of a finger reaching for the touch bar. The logo was also designed with the Atlassian brand in mind, using the same gradient colour scheme, simple geometric shapes and typography to fit the product suite. Please note that this is not an official or brand-endorsed logo and is not associated with Atlassian, but solely as an individual project.
